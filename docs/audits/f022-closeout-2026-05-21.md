## 1. Original Problem Statement

F-021 workflow inventory audit (2026-05-20) surfaced an alarming metric: **168 of 174 active workflows showed zero executions** in the n8n execution log. This appeared to indicate a near-total fleet failure ‚Äî workflows were active but not running.

F-022 was opened to triage these 168 silent workflows, determine whether they were genuinely broken or a measurement artifact, and remediate as needed.

## 2. Root Cause Identified

**Measurement artifact, not a fleet failure.**

The n8n instance (v2.14.2 at time of initial observation) was configured with `saveDataSuccessExecution=none` ‚Äî successful executions were not persisted to the execution log. Only failures were recorded. Since most workflows succeed most of the time, the execution log appeared nearly empty.

The "168 silent" count reflected missing telemetry, not missing executions.

## 3. Resolution

The root cause was already resolved before F-022 triage began:

1. **n8n upgraded** from 2.14.2 to 2.21.5 (shipped prior to F-022)
2. **`saveDataSuccessExecution` flipped to `all`** on 2026-05-13 (partial) and 2026-05-21 (full fleet)

After the flip, execution data began accumulating. Within 24 hours, 169 of 175 active workflows had recorded execution stats. The remaining 6 were webhook/form triggers that only execute when externally called ‚Äî correct behavior, not a bug.
