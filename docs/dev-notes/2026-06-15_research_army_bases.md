# U.S. Army Installations — FasTrac Signal Source Catalog
**Purpose:** Complete reference list of Army installations for GDA Command / FasTrac need-sensing (local procurement, RFIs, innovation calls)  
**Compiled for:** Envision (Alexandria, VA)  
**Last updated:** June 2025  
**Note on naming:** Several bases were renamed 2023 under the Congressional Naming Commission; a 2025 Trump executive directive reversed most of those renames (with new, non-Confederate namesakes). Current official names used below; former names in parentheses. As of June 2025, the following are active: Fort Bragg (was Fort Liberty 2023–Feb 2025), Fort Benning (was Fort Moore 2023–Mar 2025), Fort Hood (was Fort Cavazos 2023–Jun 2025), Fort Gordon (was Fort Eisenhower 2023–Jun 2025), Fort Lee (was Fort Gregg-Adams 2023–Jun 2025), Fort Polk (was Fort Johnson 2023–Jun 2025), Fort Rucker (was Fort Novosel 2023–Jun 2025), Fort Pickett (was Fort Barfoot 2023–Jun 2025), Fort Walker (was Fort Walker, name retained but now honors different individuals). See [Wikipedia renaming history](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_U.S._Army_installations_named_for_Confederate_soldiers) for full lineage.
